About the Service

What Is Digital Printing?

Digital printing is a modern printing method that transfers digital files — such as PDFs or design files — directly onto paper, card, or other substrates using high-precision inkjet or laser technology. Unlike traditional offset printing, there are no printing plates involved, which means faster setup, lower minimum quantities, and incredible color accuracy.

At AJ Traders, we use professional-grade digital printing equipment to produce sharp, vivid results on a wide range of materials. Whether you need 10 copies or 10,000, digital printing delivers consistently excellent results at a cost-effective price point.

It is ideal for time-sensitive projects, personalised prints, and any job where quality and speed are both non-negotiable.

Our Approach

How We Handle Your Print Job

A consistent, careful process ensures every print that leaves our facility meets our quality standard.

01
File Review
We check your submitted file for resolution, color mode, bleed, and safe zones before anything goes to print.
02
Digital Proof
A digital proof is sent to you for approval. We do not print until you confirm everything looks exactly right.
03
Precision Print
Your job is printed on calibrated equipment using premium inks to ensure accurate color reproduction every time.
04
Quality Check
Every batch is manually inspected for color consistency, alignment, and finish quality before it is packaged.
05
Delivery
Your prints are carefully packaged to prevent damage and delivered to your location or ready for pickup.
